Who I Am

Turning Behavior
Into Intelligence

I work at the intersection of AI, behavioral modeling, and socio-technical resilience building systems that are not only accurate but explainable and grounded in human reality.

I'm a BSc Computer Science & Engineering student at Dhaka International University (CGPA 3.76 / 4.00), graduating December 2027. My research focuses on detecting social engineering through interpretable behavioral anomaly modeling, predicting system failures using socio-economic and IoT data, and building continual learning frameworks for dynamic environments.

As President of DIU Computer Programming Club and an active IEEE & IEEE Computer Society Member, I combine research depth with community leadership championed by a NASA Space Apps 2025 victory with Team Polaris.
