Research Vision

I work at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, behavioral modeling, and socio-technical system resilience. My research focuses on:

  • Detecting social engineering through interpretable behavioral anomaly modeling
  • Predicting system failures (e.g., programmer attrition, food spoilage) using socio-economic and IoT data
  • Building continual learning frameworks for dynamic environments

I aim to develop AI systems that are not only accurate but explainable, fair, and grounded in real human behavior.
